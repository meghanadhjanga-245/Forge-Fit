Workout & Diet plans
Three fully‑defined paths: Lean (recomp), Bulky (gain) and Maintenance (balance) with training and daily nutrition.
Hydration & supplements Natural guidance
  • Water: 2.5–3.5 L per day depending on climate and activity.
  • Creatine monohydrate: 3–5 g daily for strength and performance.
  • Whey or plant protein to help hit daily protein targets.
  • Basic multivitamin and omega‑3 if dietary gaps exist.
Natural transformation rules No steroids
  • Muscle gain: ~0.25–0.5 kg/month is realistic for trained lifters.
  • Fat loss: 0.5–1% of bodyweight per week is sustainable.
  • Prioritize sleep, stress management and progressive overload.
  • Stay within 200–300 kcal surplus (bulk) or deficit (cut) for control.
Transformation timelines
Realistic expectations across 1, 3, 6 and 12 months tuned to your primary goal.

Body measurements focus
  • Track weight, chest, waist, hips, arms and legs every 1–2 weeks.
  • Use tape at the same time of day and conditions for consistency.
  • Expect faster waist changes than limb circumference during fat loss.
Mindset & expectations
  • Think in quarters (3 months), not days; compounding sessions matter most.
  • Plateaus are normal; adjust volume, intensity or calories, not effort.
  • Form, sleep and stress reduction are as important as the training plan itself.
